Help  

Usage

How to play a Cat Toy?

- Press on a one of 4 buttons in "Monitor Page".
- The toy will rotate from left to right in 120 degree aproximately.
- The number on a button means times that the toy will rotate.

How to use a cat feeder?

- Press on a button "Feed" in "Monitor Page".
- The cat feeder's lid will open and give 30g of cat food.
  (30g. = 1/8 Royal Canin's cup)

How much to feed a cat per day?

- It depends on your cat food and your cat's health.

Installation

Where should to place devices?

- Anywhere that all devices can connect to the internet (wi-fi).
- You should place all devices in same room and make sure that a camera (Main device) can focus on a cat toy and a cat feeder.
